Stuff about Alaska

Alaska is known for the beautiful sights in the sky. Not only can you witness the Northern Lights here, it gets so dark that the stars are amazing!

Stuff about terrain and life

There are many beautiful mountains all over Alaska. It is very cold there so there's a lot of snow on the mountains most of the time. Also, there are all types of animals in Alaska. There are some of the most beautiful creatures on Earth seen in Alaska. This whale is an example of those.

Camping in Alaska

Camping in Alaska can give you the whole outdoors experience. If you want to experience all of Alaska, you will have to witness the outdoors 24/7. It is incredible how beautiful it is in Alaska. It can be very cold so sleeping next to a campfire will have to be a necessity.
